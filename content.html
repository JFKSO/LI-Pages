How It Works

Step 1: Export your LinkedIn Contacts

(est. time: 5 mins)
  1. Go to your LinkedIn account settings
  2. Navigate to "Privacy" > "How LinkedIn uses your data"
  3. Click on "Get a copy of your data"
  4. Select "Connections" and request the archive

Step 2: Upload to AnonSync

(est. time: 1 min)

Upload your contacts file to our secure in-browser tool, which processes your data directly in your browser. Contact names, email addresses & timestamps are removed locally on your device before any data is shared or insights are generated.
